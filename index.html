Oil Massage

This is the practice of using essential oils to promote the physical and emotional well-being, by inhaling and or applying them to the skin.

Thai massage:

An ancient massage technique that combines yoga-like stretching with massage to promote flexibility, reduce stress, and enhance overall well-being

Swedish Massage

A Swedish massage is a classic, relaxing massage using long, flowing strokes to promote overall relaxation and well-being while soothing the body.

Hot Stone Massage

A massage that uses heated stones to relax and release tension, promote circulation and alleviate stress.

Reflexology

A massage that focuses on specific points on the feet, hands or ears, which correspond to different parts of the body, to promote healing and relaxation.

Deep Tissue Massage

A therapeutic massage that targets deep layers of muscle and connective tissue to alleviate chronic pain and tension.

Back And Shoulder Massage

A back and shoulder massage is a therapeutic massage that targets the muscles in the back and shoulders to alleviate pain, tension and stress.
